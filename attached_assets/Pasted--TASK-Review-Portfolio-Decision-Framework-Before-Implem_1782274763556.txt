## Quant Philosophy

The system is NOT a pure probability-mispricing engine.

Decision making may use:

* Probability estimation
* Market pricing analysis
* Price action
* Trend analysis
* Momentum
* Volatility
* Liquidity conditions
* Order book information
* Confidence scoring
* Opportunity scoring
* Statistical models

Probability mispricing is ONE signal only.

Future architecture must not assume all decisions originate from probability mispricing.

## Position Philosophy

Exposure Management > Position Count

Multiple entries on the same condition_id are allowed.

Duplicate entries are NOT automatically bugs.

Exposure limits are the actual constraint.

Review existing DUPLICATE_POSITION logic carefully.

---

## Exit Philosophy

A position may be closed before market settlement.

Valid exit triggers include:

* Profit target achieved
* Edge disappears
* Confidence deteriorates
* Better opportunity appears
* Exposure reduction required
* Risk conditions change

Do NOT assume:

* Hold until expiry
* Hold until settlement
* Early exits are wrong
