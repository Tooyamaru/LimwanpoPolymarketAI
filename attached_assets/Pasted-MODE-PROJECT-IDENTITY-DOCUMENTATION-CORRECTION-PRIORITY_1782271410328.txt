BUSINESS MODEL
==============

LimwanpoPolymarketAI is a prediction-market system.

The system:

1. Collects data.
2. Analyzes signals.
3. Estimates probabilities.
4. Determines whether YES or NO has statistical edge.
5. Executes YES/NO contracts on Polymarket.
6. Manages exposure.
7. Takes profit when opportunities exist.

The system predicts outcomes.

The system does NOT trade cryptocurrencies directly.

======================================================================
POSITION & EXPOSURE PHILOSOPHY
==============================

The project philosophy is:

Exposure Management > Position Count

Important:

* Multiple entries may be valid.
* Multiple positions may be valid.
* Multiple entries on the same market are not automatically bugs.
* Duplicate-position findings must first verify violation of an explicit exposure rule.

Do NOT assume:

"One market = one position"

unless explicitly defined by project rules.

======================================================================
PROFIT-TAKING PHILOSOPHY
========================

Profit-taking before settlement is valid.

Profit-taking before market expiry is valid.

Profit-taking before event resolution is valid.

Expected behavior:

Open Position
→ Opportunity Exists
→ Take Profit
→ Close Position

The system does not require waiting until event resolution.

The system does not require waiting until market expiry.

Audits must not classify early profit-taking as a defect.
