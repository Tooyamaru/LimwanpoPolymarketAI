==================================================

- log INVALID_PREDICTION_WINDOW

- jangan crash seluruh cycle

==================================================
3. EXACT MARKET BINDING
==================================================

Outcome learning harus terikat pada exact:

- condition_id
- event_slug
- prediction_window_start
- prediction_window_end

TradeDecision yang dipelajari harus cocok dengan market:

decision.condition_id == market.condition_id

Jika decision binding fields tersedia, validasi juga:

decision.decision_event_slug == market.event_slug
decision.decision_prediction_window_start == market.prediction_window_start
decision.decision_prediction_window_end == market.prediction_window_end

Decision Window A tidak boleh dipelajari memakai outcome Window B.

Jangan mencari market hanya berdasarkan asset.

==================================================
4. OFFICIAL OUTCOME ONLY
==================================================

Gunakan hanya official resolved outcome yang sudah tersedia melalui existing
Polymarket resolution pathway.

Jangan:

- menebak hasil dari GAP
- memakai current Chainlink price sebagai settlement outcome
- memakai CLOB midpoint sebagai hasil
- membuat fallback UP/DOWN
- melakukan manual DB status update
- memakai synthetic 0.5

Jika official outcome belum tersedia:

- status tetap RESOLVING/pending sesuai existing design
- jangan membuat learning record
- jangan settle PnL
- retry pada cycle berikutnya

==================================================
5. DUPLICATE PROTECTION
==================================================

Pastikan satu decision/market tidak dipelajari dua kali.

Gunakan existing idempotency mechanism jika sudah ada.

Required:

- satu condition + decision menghasilkan maksimal satu learning record
- repeated worker cycle tidak menggandakan learning
- repeated official resolution tidak menggandakan settlement
- jangan mengubah realized PnL dua kali

Jangan membuat mekanisme paralel baru jika constraint/idempotency existing
sudah tersedia.
