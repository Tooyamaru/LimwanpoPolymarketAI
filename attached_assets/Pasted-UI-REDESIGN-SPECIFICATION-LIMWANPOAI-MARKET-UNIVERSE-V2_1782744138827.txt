CARD COLOR RULES

Cards must immediately communicate their condition.

Do not rely only on text.

Use border, glow and subtle background color.

GREEN

Position active and currently profitable.

RED

Position active and currently losing.

YELLOW

No position.
AI is WATCH or READY.

GRAY

No opportunity.
WAIT.

BLUE

Current AI Target (optional highlight).

The operator should understand the portfolio within one second simply by looking at colors.
