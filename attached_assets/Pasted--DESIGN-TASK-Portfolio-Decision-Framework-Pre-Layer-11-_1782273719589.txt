# Design Deliverable

Design a new component:

Portfolio Decision Engine

Purpose:

Evaluate the entire portfolio every cycle.

Input:

* Open positions
* Opportunity scores
* Confidence metrics
* Liquidity conditions
* Order book state
* Exposure state
* Risk state

Output:

Portfolio decisions.

Possible outcomes:

* KEEP_POSITION
* ADD_TO_POSITION
* REDUCE_POSITION
* ROTATE_POSITION
* CLOSE_POSITION

These are portfolio-management decisions.

Execution actions are generated afterward.

---

# Portfolio Decision Philosophy

The system must NOT use:

if pnl > target:
close()

as the sole exit mechanism.

Decisions must be multi-factor.

Potential factors:

* Opportunity score
* Confidence score
* Probability estimate
* Market pricing
* Liquidity
* Spread quality
* Order book imbalance
* Exposure utilization
* Risk utilization
* Relative ranking versus other opportunities

No single factor may be the sole criterion.

# Polymarket Market Mechanics

Use Polymarket mechanics only.

Important:

YES and NO contracts are traded instruments.

Bid and ask prices matter.

Do not assume execution at midpoint.

When discussing live execution:

* Buy YES at ask
* Sell YES at bid
* Buy NO at ask
* Sell NO at bid

Mid prices may be used for analytics but not assumed execution.
