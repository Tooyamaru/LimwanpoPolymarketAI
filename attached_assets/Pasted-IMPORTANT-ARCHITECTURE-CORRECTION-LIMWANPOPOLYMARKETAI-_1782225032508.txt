IMPORTANT ARCHITECTURE CORRECTION — LIMWANPOPOLYMARKETAI

Before performing any audit, refactor, bug analysis, roadmap planning, architecture review, or Layer 11 implementation, you MUST understand the business model correctly.

PROJECT IDENTITY

LimwanpoPolymarketAI is NOT a crypto trading bot.

LimwanpoPolymarketAI is a Polymarket Quant Bot.

The system predicts outcomes in Polymarket prediction markets and executes YES/NO contracts based on quantitative signals.

CRITICAL DISTINCTION

Do NOT treat this system as:

* Binance Futures Bot
* Binance Spot Trading Bot
* Arbitrage Bot
* Market Making Bot
* Traditional Trading System

Instead treat it as:

* Prediction Market Engine
* Polymarket Quant System
* Outcome Probability Engine
* YES/NO Contract Execution System

ASSET INTERPRETATION

BTC, ETH, SOL, and BNB are NOT traded assets.

BTC, ETH, SOL, and BNB are prediction inputs only.

External data sources such as Binance, Chainlink, market feeds, sentiment feeds, and price feeds are used solely to estimate probabilities of Polymarket outcomes.

The actual instruments being traded are:

* YES contracts
* NO contracts

on Polymarket.

EXAMPLE

Correct interpretation:

Signal Engine
→ Predict BTC Up probability
→ Decide YES or NO
→ Buy YES/NO contract on Polymarket

Incorrect interpretation:

Signal Engine
→ Buy BTC
→ Hold BTC
→ Sell BTC

The system NEVER trades BTC directly.

BUSINESS RULES

1. Multiple entries on the same market are allowed.
2. Multiple positions on the same condition_id are allowed if exposure rules permit them.
3. Exposure management is more important than position count.
4. Duplicate entries must NOT automatically be classified as bugs.
5. Profit-taking exits before market settlement are valid and preferred.
6. Positions do NOT need to remain open until event resolution.
7. Closing a profitable position before expiry is expected behavior.
8. Audits must distinguish prediction-market behavior from traditional trading behavior.
9. Findings based on spot/futures assumptions are invalid unless explicitly applicable to prediction markets.
10. Any architecture recommendation must align with Polymarket market mechanics.

AUDIT RULES

When reviewing code:

* Do not assume one position per market.
* Do not assume positions remain open until expiry.
* Do not assume BTC/ETH/SOL/BNB are traded instruments.
* Do not assume futures exchange behavior.
* Do not assume spot exchange behavior.

Always evaluate findings against Polymarket prediction-market logic first.
