==================================================
1. REPLACE "EXPIRES" CLOCK WITH LIVE COUNTDOWN
==================================================

The current UI displays:

Expires
22:18:45

This is incorrect.

Prediction market traders care about remaining time, NOT the closing clock time.

Replace it with a live countdown.

Examples:

⏳ 2h 18m
⏳ 45m
⏳ 8m 31s
⏳ 42s

Rules:

Remaining > 1 hour
→ 2h 18m

Remaining < 1 hour
→ 43m

Remaining < 10 minutes
→ 8m 31s

Remaining < 1 minute
→ 42s

When market expires:

RESOLVED

or

Expired

The countdown must update every second.

Never display the wall-clock closing time again.
