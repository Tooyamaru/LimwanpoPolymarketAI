PART C — VERIFY OUT EVENTS IN AI LIVE TRADES
==================================================

Audit GET /api/v1/live-trades.

Feed harus memasukkan real executed exit events:

- PARTIAL_EXIT
- FINAL_EXIT
- EXPIRY_EXIT

Contoh display:

06:04:12  OUT  BTC 15M  DOWN  $6.40 @ 0.540  PNL +$0.80

06:05:27  OUT  XRP 1H   UP    $10.80 @ 0.540  PNL +$0.80

06:03:41  PARTIAL OUT  BTC 5M  DOWN  $5.20  PNL +$0.20

06:10:00  EXPIRY OUT  ETH 5M  UP  $20.00  PNL +$10.00

Audit source:

- exit order status FILLED
- close_decision_id
- close_order_id
- close_quantity
- exit_price
- realized_pnl
- close_reason
- remaining_quantity
- position status

Pastikan endpoint tidak hanya membaca entry orders.

Classify event:

ENTRY:
- first executed entry

SCALE_IN:
- additional executed entry same condition_id

PARTIAL_EXIT:
- close_quantity < previous remaining_quantity

FINAL_EXIT:
- remaining_quantity becomes 0

EXPIRY_EXIT:
- close_reason EXPIRY / RESOLUTION

Forbidden in feed:

- WAIT
- BLOCKED
- PENDING
- REJECTED
- generic decision
- signal
- opportunity
- heartbeat

Optional:
EXECUTION_REJECTED boleh ada hanya jika benar-benar dibutuhkan,
tetapi default feed fokus ke executed IN/OUT.
