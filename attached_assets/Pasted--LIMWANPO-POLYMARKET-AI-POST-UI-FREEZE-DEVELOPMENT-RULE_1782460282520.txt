# Engine Responsibilities

## Market Scanner

Collect raw market data.

Examples:

* Best Bid
* Best Ask
* Spread
* Liquidity
* Orderbook
* Recent Odds Changes
* Market Activity

Scanner DOES NOT make decisions.

---

## Market Evaluation

Convert raw data into standardized scores.

Examples:

Spread Score

Liquidity Score

Momentum Score

Execution Score

Odds Stability Score

Market Quality Score

↓

Scanner Score

Every market receives a real evaluation.

---

## Opportunity Ranking

Sort every monitored market.

Example:

BTC 5m

92

ETH 15m

87

SOL 1H

81

This engine powers:

* Heatmap
* Top Market
* Rankings

---

## Confidence Engine

Estimate probability that an opportunity is worth trading.

Produces:

Confidence Score

Confidence Trend

Confidence Change

Confidence Reason

---

## Decision Engine

Makes the trading decision.

Produces:

Selected Market

Reason

Entry Readiness

Wait / Skip / Enter

This engine should also explain WHY.

Example:

Momentum Rising

Spread Acceptable

Liquidity Good

Risk Acceptable

Confidence Increasing

---

## Risk Engine

Calculate:

Maximum Position

Maximum Loss

Risk Score

Position Size

Stop Conditions

Capital Protection

---

## Paper Trading Engine

Execute simulated trades.

Track:

Entry

Exit

PnL

Fees

Win Rate

Holding Time

Risk

Everything must behave like a real trader.

---

## Performance Analytics

Generate statistics.

Examples:

Win Rate

Average Return

Drawdown

Sharpe-like Metrics

Strategy Comparison

Market Comparison

Timeframe Comparison

Confidence Accuracy

Decision Accuracy

---

## Continuous AI Learning

Continuously improve from paper trading history.

Learn:

Which setups work.

Which setups fail.

Improve ranking.

Improve confidence.

Improve decision quality.
