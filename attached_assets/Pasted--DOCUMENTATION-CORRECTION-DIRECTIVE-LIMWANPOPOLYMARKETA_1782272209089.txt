# IMPORTANT PROJECT IDENTITY

LimwanpoPolymarketAI is a specialized project inside the broader LimwanpoAI ecosystem.

LimwanpoPolymarketAI is a:

**Polymarket Quant Bot**

It is NOT:

* A Binance trading bot
* A Spot trading bot
* A Futures trading bot
* A Scalping bot
* A Market making bot
* An Arbitrage bot
* A Copy trading bot

The system exists to identify statistical and probabilistic opportunities in Polymarket prediction markets.

---

# CORE BUSINESS MODEL

The purpose of LimwanpoPolymarketAI is NOT to trade BTC, ETH, SOL, or XRP.

The purpose is to trade:

* YES contracts
* NO contracts

on Polymarket prediction markets.

BTC, ETH, SOL, and XRP are only market themes and prediction subjects.

Examples:

* Will BTC be above a specified price at a specified time?
* Will ETH close above a specified level?
* Will SOL exceed a target price?
* Will XRP remain below a threshold?

The traded instrument is always:

YES or NO contracts.

Never the underlying crypto asset.

---

# ROLE OF BINANCE AND CHAINLINK

Binance and Chainlink are NOT trading venues.

Binance and Chainlink are signal sources only.

Their purpose is:

* Price discovery
* Trend analysis
* Volatility analysis
* Probability estimation
* Quantitative signal generation

The system must never be described as trading on Binance.

The system must never execute orders on Binance.

The final execution venue is Polymarket.

---

# QUANT PHILOSOPHY

The system is a Quant Bot.

The objective is to identify pricing inefficiencies between:

A. The probability implied by Polymarket prices

and

B. The probability estimated by LimwanpoPolymarketAI models

Example:

Polymarket YES price implies:

40% probability

Internal model estimates:

68% probability

Edge:

+28%

This difference creates a candidate opportunity.

The system therefore searches for probabilistic edge, not simple directional price movement.

Avoid reducing the project description to:

"BTC naik → BUY YES"

or

"BTC turun → BUY NO"

because that does not accurately describe the quantitative model.

---

# POSITION PHILOSOPHY

Multiple positions may be valid.

Position count alone is not the primary control mechanism.

Exposure management is the primary control mechanism.

Documentation must not assume:

"One market = one position"

unless explicitly stated by source code.

Documentation must not classify multiple entries as bugs without verifying actual exposure-management rules.

# PROFIT-TAKING PHILOSOPHY

LimwanpoPolymarketAI is not designed to hold every position until market settlement.

Early exits are valid.

Profit-taking before event resolution is valid.

Closing positions before expiry is valid.

A profitable exit before settlement should not be classified as an error.

All future audits must evaluate position management using prediction-market logic.
