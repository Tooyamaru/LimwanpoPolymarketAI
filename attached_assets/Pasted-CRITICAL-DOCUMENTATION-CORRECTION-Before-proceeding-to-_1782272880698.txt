* YES contracts

* BTC
* ETH
* SOL
* XRP

==================================================
QUANT PHILOSOPHY
================

The objective is NOT simply to predict whether BTC will go up or down.

The objective is to identify high-probability profitable opportunities on Polymarket using quantitative analysis.

Decision making may include:

* Price action
* Trend analysis
* Momentum analysis
* Volatility analysis
* Historical behavior
* Statistical models
* Confidence scoring
* Opportunity scoring
* Probability estimation
* Market pricing analysis

Probability mispricing is one signal.

It is NOT the only signal.

Future development must not assume that all decisions originate solely from probability mispricing calculations.

==================================================
POSITION PHILOSOPHY
===================

Multiple positions may exist simultaneously.

Multiple entries on the same market may be valid.

Exposure management is more important than position count.

Do not assume:

"One market = one position"

unless explicitly defined by future risk rules.

==================================================
EXIT PHILOSOPHY
===============

Positions do NOT need to remain open until settlement.

Profit-taking before event resolution is valid.

Early exits are expected behavior.

The system should be capable of:

* Opening positions
* Scaling positions
* Reducing positions
* Closing positions before settlement
