9. VERIFY ACCOUNTING ENDPOINT
==================================================

Pastikan endpoint berikut tersedia tepat satu kali:

GET /api/v1/portfolio/accounting

Expected response:

- initial_capital
- portfolio_open_lots
- portfolio_partial_lots
- portfolio_active_lots
- portfolio_active_positions
- open_exposure
- total_realized_pnl
- total_unrealized_pnl
- raw_available_capital
- spendable_available_capital
- cumulative_outcome
- total_entry_fills
- total_exit_fills

Formulas:

portfolio_active_lots =
COUNT positions
WHERE status IN ('OPEN', 'PARTIAL')

open_exposure =
SUM(remaining_quantity × entry_price)
WHERE status IN ('OPEN', 'PARTIAL')

raw_available_capital =
initial_capital + total_realized_pnl - open_exposure

cumulative_outcome =
total_realized_pnl + total_unrealized_pnl

Frontend global metrics harus memakai endpoint ini.
