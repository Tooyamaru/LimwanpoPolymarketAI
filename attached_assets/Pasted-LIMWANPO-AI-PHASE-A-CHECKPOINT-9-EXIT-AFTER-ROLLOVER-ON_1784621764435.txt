3. USE PREDICTION WINDOW END
==================================================

Untuk market 5M gunakan hanya:

market.prediction_window_end

now >= prediction_window_end

posisi masuk jalur expiry/resolution handling.

Jangan menggunakan:

market.end_time
market.start_time
prediction_window_end or end_time
contract lifecycle

- jangan fallback ke end_time
- jangan membuat fake exit
- log INVALID_PREDICTION_WINDOW
- posisi tetap dikelola untuk recovery/official resolution
- jangan crash seluruh worker

4. EXIT MUST NOT REQUIRE WINDOW_LIVE
==================================================

Exit/close handling harus tetap berjalan ketika lifecycle market:

RESOLVING
RESOLVED
INVALID metadata recovery

Jangan menambahkan gate:

prediction_lifecycle_state == WINDOW_LIVE

WINDOW_LIVE hanya untuk entry, bukan exit.

==================================================

- current card disappearance tidak menghapus posisi lama

Jangan mengalihkan posisi lama ke market baru berdasarkan asset.
