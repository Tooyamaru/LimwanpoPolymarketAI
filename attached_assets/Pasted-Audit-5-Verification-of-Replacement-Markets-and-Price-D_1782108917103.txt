Audit #5 – Verification of Replacement Markets and Price Discovery

IMPORTANT:

Do NOT assume previous conclusions are correct.

Previous audits proved:

* Original 5m/15m markets stayed frozen.
* No real-time linkage to Binance was detected.
* Depth updates appeared scheduled.
* Original contracts expired and rolled to new token IDs.

However, new evidence has appeared:

* Replacement BTC/5m market observed around mid ≈ 0.875.
* This contradicts the hypothesis that all markets remain permanently at 0.50.

The purpose of this audit is to determine whether:

A) Market prices are actually formed by traders.

B) Markets are repriced only during creation/rollover.

C) AMM performs hidden pricing updates.

D) Previous conclusions were artifacts of observing expired markets.
