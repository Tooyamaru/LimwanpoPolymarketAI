==================================================
PART B — RESTORE COUNTDOWN NEXT TO TIMEFRAME
==================================================

Countdown harus dikembalikan tanpa menambah row baru.

Target tampilan:

BTC  5M · 04:32

atau:

BTC  5M  04:32

Untuk 15M:

BTC  15M · 12:18

Untuk 1H:

BTC  1H · 48:09

Letakkan countdown tepat di sebelah badge timeframe:

[BTC] [5M] [04:32]

Jangan meletakkan countdown kembali di bagian bawah card.

==================================================
COUNTDOWN RULES
==================================================

Gunakan lifecycle state dan timestamp resmi:

PRE_MARKET:
- countdown menuju start_time
- prefix opsional: OPENS 04:32

ACTIVE:
- countdown menuju end_time
- tampilkan format ringkas:
  - MM:SS jika kurang dari 1 jam
  - HH:MM jika 1 jam atau lebih

EXPIRED:
- tampilkan EXPIRED
- jangan countdown negatif

RESOLUTION_PENDING:
- tampilkan PENDING

RESOLVED:
- tampilkan RESOLVED

INVALID_TIME_STATE:
- tampilkan —

Gunakan UTC internal, tetapi countdown berupa durasi sehingga tidak perlu menampilkan timezone.

Pastikan timer update setiap 1 detik tanpa reload seluruh card jika memungkinkan.

==================================================
COUNTDOWN SAFETY
==================================================

Countdown harus berasal dari:

- market.start_time
- market.end_time
- lifecycle_state

Jangan berasal dari:

- timeframe label saja
- hardcoded 5/15/60 menit
- tanggal parsing dari title jika field resmi tersedia
- shared timer satu card untuk semua condition_id

Setiap condition_id harus punya target timestamp sendiri.

==================================================
PART E — IDEMPOTENCY VS VALID ADDITIONAL ENTRY
==================================================

Bedakan dengan tegas:

INVALID_DUPLICATE_EXECUTION:
- trade_decision.id sama
- order.id sama
- execution_key sama
- retry request yang sama
- event yang sama diproses dua kali

VALID_ADDITIONAL_ENTRY:
- decision_id baru
- risk approval baru
- timestamp baru
- market masih ACTIVE
- cooldown selesai
- exposure belum melewati limit
- side sesuai aturan
- quantity valid

Duplicate guard harus memakai:

- source_decision_id
- source_order_id
- execution_key

Jangan memakai condition_id sebagai idempotency key.

PART G — SCALE-IN RULES
==================================================

Entry tambahan hanya valid jika:

- lifecycle_state == ACTIVE
- execution_allowed == true
- orderbook fresh
- market tidak expired
- decision baru
- risk approval baru
- cooldown selesai
- exposure cap aman
- opportunity/confidence masih memenuhi aturan
- quantity > 0
- tidak duplicate execution

Simpan scale-in reason:

- SIGNAL_STRENGTHENED
- CONFIDENCE_INCREASED
- BETTER_ENTRY_PRICE
- ORDER_FLOW_CONFIRMED
- PARTIAL_ALLOCATION
- REBALANCE
- REENTRY_AFTER_PARTIAL_EXIT

Jangan scale-in tanpa reason.

==================================================
PART H — SAME SIDE / OPPOSITE SIDE
==================================================

Default requirement:

- multiple LONG_YES lots: allowed
- multiple LONG_NO lots: allowed
- LONG_YES + LONG_NO bersamaan: blocked by default

Jika opposite side ditemukan dan hedge disabled:

- reject dengan OPPOSITE_SIDE_CONFLICT

Jika hedge mode diaktifkan suatu hari nanti:
- harus explicit setting
- jangan aktif default

==================================================
PART I — PARTIAL EXIT
==================================================

Implement partial exit.

Support:

- close quantity tertentu
- close satu lot
- close beberapa lot
- close semua remaining lots
- force close saat expiry

Gunakan policy jelas:

Default:
FIFO

Fields yang harus tercatat:

- position_id / lot_id
- close_quantity
- exit_price
- exit_notional
- realized_pnl
- remaining_quantity
- exit_reason
- closed_at

Rules:

Jika remaining_quantity > 0:
- status PARTIAL atau OPEN sesuai model

Jika remaining_quantity == 0:
- status CLOSED

Tidak boleh close lebih besar dari remaining_quantity.

==================================================
PART J — EXPIRY EXIT FOR MULTIPLE LOTS
==================================================

Saat market expired/resolved:

- cari semua remaining OPEN/PARTIAL lots pada condition_id
- force-close seluruh remaining quantity

LONG_YES:
- forced_exit_price = final_yes_price

LONG_NO:
- forced_exit_price = final_no_price

Hitung realized PnL per lot.

Jangan:

- memakai stale CLOB
- hanya menutup satu lot
- meninggalkan lot lain OPEN
- double-close lot yang sudah closed

==================================================
PART M — IN / OUT RULES
==================================================

IN count:

jumlah executed entry events:

- OPEN_LONG_YES
- OPEN_LONG_NO

IN amount:

sum actual entry notional:

entry_price × quantity

OUT count:

jumlah executed exit events / partial exit events

OUT amount:

sum actual exit notional:

exit_price × close_quantity

Jangan menghitung:

- WAIT
- WATCH
- BLOCKED
- REJECTED
- FAILED
- PENDING
- duplicate retry
- decision yang belum executed
