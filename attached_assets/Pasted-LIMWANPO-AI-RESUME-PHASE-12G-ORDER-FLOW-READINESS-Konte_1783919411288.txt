==================================================
STEP 2 — IMPLEMENT MARKET ACTIVITY CLASSIFIER
==================================================

Buat:

backend/app/services/market_activity_classifier.py

State:

- SEED_ONLY
- BOOK_CHANGED
- PRICE_MOVED
- DEPTH_CHANGED
- TRADED
- HIGH_ACTIVITY

Input:

- previous snapshot
- current snapshot
- volume
- liquidity
- bid/ask/mid
- spread
- bid/ask sizes/depth jika tersedia
- timestamp

Rules harus deterministik dan berbasis real data.

Contoh:

SEED_ONLY:
- no recent trade
- no volume
- bid/ask/mid/spread unchanged

BOOK_CHANGED:
- bid atau ask berubah
- mid belum berubah signifikan

PRICE_MOVED:
- mid berubah melewati threshold setting

DEPTH_CHANGED:
- bid/ask size atau total depth berubah signifikan

TRADED:
- volume atau last trade timestamp meningkat

HIGH_ACTIVITY:
- multiple changes dalam short window

Jangan infer TRADED hanya dari bid/ask change.
