STEP 4 — TEST DIRECT RESOLUTION LOGIC
=====================================

Pastikan test mencakup minimal:

1. Gamma client:

* closed market dengan outcomePrices YES=1, NO=0 → winning_side YES
* closed market dengan outcomePrices YES=0, NO=1 → winning_side NO
* live market dengan outcomePrices 0.51/0.49 → NOT_AVAILABLE / not resolved
* missing outcomePrices → NOT_AVAILABLE
* condition_id mismatch → reject
* token_id mismatch → reject

2. Outcome Learning:

* BUY_YES + winning_side YES → correct=True
* BUY_YES + winning_side NO → correct=False
* BUY_NO + winning_side NO → correct=True
* BUY_NO + winning_side YES → correct=False
* direct resolution unavailable + realized_pnl positive → correct=True with outcome_source REALIZED_PNL_PROXY
* direct resolution unavailable + realized_pnl negative → correct=False with outcome_source REALIZED_PNL_PROXY
* already_evaluated → skip
* expired + end_time past → evaluate
* future market → skip

3. Repository:

* outcome_source persists
* winning_side persists
* winning_token_id persists
* accuracy/correctness persists
* old rows remain readable
